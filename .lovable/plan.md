# S2G Marketing Video Plan

## Goal
Build awareness of Sow2Grow across TikTok, Facebook, X, and YouTube, explaining what the platform is, what users can do, and how anyone can generate income.

## Recommendation: 12–14 core videos, then 20+ platform-specific cuts

You already have 40+ internal explainers on Learn & Share. This plan is for **new, externally-facing marketing videos** designed for conversion and shareability.

### Phase 1: Awareness & Positioning (5 videos)

| # | Title | Length | Style | Purpose |
|---|-------|--------|-------|---------|
| 1 | **What is Sow2Grow?** | 60–90s | Polished motion graphics | Hero video. Defines S2G as a global tribal CaaS marketplace where seeds become orchards. |
| 2 | **The Tribe Economy in 60 Seconds** | 60s | Polished | Explains the circular value flow: sowers create, bestowers support, whisperers amplify. |
| 3 | **What Can You Actually Do on S2G?** | 90s | Fast montage | Showcases orchards, live rooms, wandering directory, calendar, wallet, community videos. |
| 4 | **Sower, Bestower, Whisperer** | 60s | Clean split-screen | The three primary ways to participate and belong. |
| 5 | **Your Seed Becomes an Orchard** | 45s | Visual metaphor | Walks the idea from a single seed to a funded/growing community orchard. |

### Platform Distribution

From the 14 core videos, produce derivative cuts:

- **YouTube**: 14 full-length explainers (landscape, SEO titles/descriptions)
- **TikTok / Reels / Shorts**: 14 vertical cuts, 15–30s each, with hooks in first 3s
- **Facebook**: 8–10 community-focused 30–60s cuts
- **X / Twitter**: 10–12 short 30–45s cuts with bold captions

**Total video assets: ~34–40**

## Production Approach

- **5 polished hero videos** (What is S2G, Tribe Economy, Sower/Bestower/Whisperer, Wandering Directory, Income overview) — use motion graphics / AI narration.
- **9 quick founder/screen-capture videos** for feature and income explainers — authentic, faster to produce.
- All videos use consistent S2G branding: dark garden aesthetic, teal/ember/gold palette, Fraunces/Outfit typography.

## Suggested Rollout Order

1. Launch **Video 1 (What is S2G?)** + **Video 2 (Tribe Economy)** as the foundation.
2. Release **Videos 3–5** over the following 2 weeks.
3. Drop **Feature Deep-Dives 6–9** one per week.
4. Release **Income videos 10–14** as a "Earn on S2G" mini-series.
5. Repurpose every core video into platform-specific cuts using the first 5–10 seconds as the hook.
